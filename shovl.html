Introduction

“SHOVL”, which is a C2C snow clearing service. Our potential clients are busy working professionals, senior citizens or anyone who is not able to clear/shovel the snow after a heavy snow deposit due to snowstorm/snowfall. Using our mobile application users who are unable to clear the snow due to any reason do not need to bother about it anymore. They can get the service done in cheap rates and within the clicks of few buttons.

Role in Project

This was a group project of 5 people. We all are doing all the activities in project, so everyone can learn all the topics.
