Introduction

Umore is an app that lets you track when and how you are most likely to get stressed. See graphs to easily visualize what events are making you lose your cool
-Daily quiz to determine how stressful your day is.
-Analyse which days of the week are most stressful
-Analyse which time of day is most stressful
-Visual representation and aids to
-Suggestions to improve mental health during stressful parts of the day
-Quick access to emergency service phone lines depending on region
